Who Am I ?

You might have seen me before... sitting quietly in the grass, doing absolutely nothing. Or atleast, that's what it looks like. Because I don't really move much. I just watch.

I am Meowster🐾. I don't chase, I don't make noise, and I don't like unnecessary attention. Some people say black cats bring bad luck. I don't argue with them... It makes things more interesting.

I appear when I want, disappear when I'm bored, and somehow end up exactly where I'm not expected to be. Maybe I'm just a cat. Or maybe there's more than you think.
